Best Massages &amp; Spas in Jakarta

April 2022 · 5 minute read

About Jakarta

The capital city of Indonesia seems to never sleep. It is bustling from morning to evening, full of workers and travelers. Jakarta is the main destination for Indonesians to find works, study, and travel. Massage and spa in Jakarta are available in abundance, relieving stress and exhaustion from its citizens and visitors. Jakarta has every type of travel destination you can think of. Families can visit Ancol Theme Park, Ragunan Zoo, and Taman Mini Indonesia Indah Park. Adventurers can visit Seribu Island Regency to try island hopping, diving, and snorkeling. Jakarta is also full of historical and educational sites, such as the National Museum, National Monument (Monas), and Kota Tua (Oldtown). Banteng Field and Bung Karno Forest are cheap destinations for backpackers. Jakarta also has numerous culinary spots, such as Kelapa Gading, Pecenongan, Kemang, Bendungan Hilir, and Sabang Street. You can find both hawker-type foods and luxurious distro dishes. Exploring destinations in Jakarta will drain you after a while, so visiting a spa can be a relaxing closure for the day.

Jakarta Spa Review

Spa is an important part of the urban lifestyle in Jakarta. Many spa establishments are situated near business or lifestyle centers, giving urban citizens great places to relax and unwind. You can find all types of spa establishments in Jakarta, such as Balinese, Japanese, Chinese, Javanese, and family spas. There are several common services you can find at various spa establishments in Jakarta, such as

Traditional Massage

Traditional massage is one of the most favorite services among spa visitors in Jakarta. Indonesian-style massage incorporates gentle, full-body treatment with traditional or aromatic oil. The treatment is relaxing and perfect to relieve stress.

Reflexology

Based on Ancient Chinese treatment, reflexology offers gentle massage and slight pressures on specific spots on your feet and hands. These spots are believed to affect certain internal organs and bodily functions.

Maternity Massage

Maternity massage is growing in popularity in big cities like Jakarta. Pregnant women, mothers, and babies can enjoy various health benefits from professional therapists. The spa establishments have a relaxing and comforting atmosphere for such customers.

Martha Tilaar Salon Day Spa

Maintained under one of Indonesia’s beauty product giants, Martha Tilaar Salon Day Spa has several branches in Jakarta’s popular areas, like Pluit, Pondok Indah, Gandaria, Kuningan, and Cikini. You can enjoy full-body treatments using high-quality products with Martha Tillar’s iconic formula.

Recommended packages include Traditional Body Massage, Acupressure, and Face Mask. Dewi Sri packages offer skin and beauty treatments, like Body Scrub and Body Bliss Spa Whitening Package. Prices range from Rp205.750 to Rp375.000 per person.

Mom N Jo

Mom N Jo is the pioneer of maternity massage in Jakarta, serving pregnant women, mothers, babies, and toddlers. You can find its branches in several areas, such as Bintaro, Kelapa Gading, Plaza Indonesia, Jatiwaringin, and Kalimalang. Several of them are situated in shopping centers, making it convenient to visit.

The Jump Start package is available to stimulate the sensory and motoric skills, available for 1 to 12-month-old babies. Older kids can try a fun chocolate bath. Mothers and pregnant women can try Gadget Holic Treatment, which focuses on eyes, neck, shoulders, and back areas. You can also try special massage treatments for pregnancy and postnatal. Prices range from Rp161.000 to Rp525.000.

Nest Family Reflexology and Spa

Nest Family Reflexology and Spa has branches in Bintaro, Lippo Mall Puri, and Baywalk Mall Pluit. It offers traditional Indonesian treatments for relaxation and rejuvenation. All therapists are well-trained and certificated. Favorite treatments include Javanese Massage and Balinese Massage, which offer full-body treatments using traditional ingredients. Face Acupressure, Relaxing Experience, and Massage Reflexology are available to remove your exhaustion. Foot Reflexology and Back & Head Massage are recommended after a long day of exploring Jakarta.

Want to try a different massage experience? Full Body Tuina is a Chinese-style body manipulation treatment that combines several techniques in one package. Prices range from Rp115.000 to Rp250.000.

Zen Spa

Zeb Spa Jakarta has two branches in Pakubuwono and Gunawarman. They are close to business areas, making them popular among office workers. Zen Spa offers series of treatments that relax your body and refresh your mind.

You can book a full-body Balinese massage from experienced therapists for ultimate indulgence. Other favorite packages include Indulgence Foot Relaxing, Foot Yoga Reflexology, Secluded Relaxation Massage, and Hot Yoga Massage. Prices range from Rp89.100 to Rp306.000.

Saffron Spa and Hammam

Situated at Melati Street 3, Ampera Raya, Saffron Spa and Hammam is a great place to spend a relaxing day. Enjoy a series of spa treatments to refresh and rejuvenate your mind, skin, and body.

Moroccan Hammam and Gommage Treatment are popular packages at this spa establishment. They are parts of traditional full-body treatments in Morocco and Korea, brought to Jakarta by experienced therapists. The prices for packages at this spa range from Rp360.000 to Rp450.000.

Bersih Sehat Pondok Indah

Bersih Sehat is one of the pioneers of affordable massage establishments in Jakarta. The place is simple but comfortable, with Javanese design elements in the furniture. It focuses on traditional full-body massage and scrubbing with natural ingredients. The effect is refreshing and rejuvenating. Prices range from Rp94.000 to Rp342.500.
