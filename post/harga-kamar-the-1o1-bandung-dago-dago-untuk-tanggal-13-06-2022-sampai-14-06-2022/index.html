Description / About The Hotel

Conveniently located in the legendary of Dago, THE 1O1 Bandung Dago is a great base to explore this vibrant city. Less than 5 km away, this 4-star hotel can be easily accessed from The Husein Sastranegara Airport and Bandung Train Station. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city’s must-see destinations such as Shopping Malls, Factory Outlets, Entertainments, Restaurants and Tourist Attractions.

THE 1O1 Bandung Dago Hotel also offers many facilities to enrich your stay in Bandung. Featuring eclectic interiors and unique room concepts, the hotel include 24-hour room service, Dutch Blue Pool (kids preference), Whales Spa & Massage, Valet Service, and Free Wifi in all rooms.

Although smoking is not permitted in hotel rooms themselves, guests who smoke are permitted to do outside designated areas. There is a significant room recovery fee for guests who do not comply in order to cover the extensive cost of restoring rooms to a smoke-free condition and safety reason.

Culinary experience at Bandung you will find SODA Resto & Bar. Heritage and Fusion dishes that satisfy guest cravings. All menus are available with affordable price ! Let’s have fun with SODAkustik or Live DJ Performance at Saturday night…
